Central Dogma Decoder

In this game, you can enter a strand of DNA code, and then it will decode it for you. First, it translates and displays the DNA to mRNA code, then the tRNA code, and lastly, the letter code. Watch over your shoulder, though, because the world's security depends on only you having this machine.

Planet Discovery: Emission Match

In this game, you are racing against other astronomers to identify the elements in a planet's atmosphere. You must match the order of the displayed emission spectra of elements in the planet's atmosphere by entering the element symbol. You don't get to look at the spectra for long, but if you are successful, you can name the planet. Enjoy!

Photosynthesis Tycoon

In this game, you are a plant trying to collect as much energy as possible. To do this, you must photosynthesize. Collect water, carbon dioxide, and sunlight using your energy. Use your resources and leaves to create more energy. Grow and expand your plant, but stay energized, or you lose.
